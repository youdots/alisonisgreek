Operation AlisonIsGreek

Greek costume strategy for Ibiza. Target: 42-year-old male. Timeline: end of next week. Focus: fast assembly, breathable fabrics, high visual impact.

Core Builds (DIY + Fast Shipping)

Classic Toga / Philosopher

  • DIY: bed sheet or large fabric, rope belt, sandals.
  • Props: laurel wreath, scroll, fake beard.
  • Local: EIVITOYS, Tur Toy. Online: Funidelia, Disfrazzes.

Zeus / Poseidon

  • Zeus: white toga + lightning bolt. Poseidon: teal tunic + trident.
  • Upgrade: gold cuffs, shell accents, net cape.
  • Disfrazzes + Funidelia ship 24-48h to Balearics.

Spartan Warrior

  • Short tunic, red cape, leather wristbands.
  • Props: foam shield, spear, bronze paint.
  • Craft stores for EVA foam; Disfrazzes for base costume.

Dionysus

  • Wine-colored toga, ivy crown, grape clusters.
  • Props: gold goblet, faux fur, gold chains.
  • Local: Toy Planet, Don Dino for accessories.

Satyr / Pan

  • Brown leggings, faux fur, horn headpiece.
  • Prop: simple flute.
  • Local: EIVITOYS, Tur Toy for horns and wigs.

Greek Philosopher

  • Simple toga, scroll, fake beard.
  • Prop upgrade: chalkboard with a quote.
  • Funidelia basic tunic delivery in 48h.
